About Harry Potter

Harry Potter is a fictional character and the hero of the wizarding world of Harry Potter, created by J.K. Rowling. He is an English half-blood wizard. He is the only son of James Potter and Lily Potter (nee Evans) and the godson of Sirius Black. He was born on July 31, 1980. Harry Potter attended the Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardy. He was an accomplished Quidditch star and perfect at defending against the dark arts, having been able to create a physical patronus at the age of 13. He grew up to quit Voldemort in 1998. He assumed a position as head of the Auror's office and head of magical law enforcement. He was and is a man who has a crush on a large audience. His wife is Ginevra Molly / Ginny Potter (nee Weasley)
